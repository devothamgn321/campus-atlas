# LinkedIn Post Draft

I built CampusOS as an AI Product Manager portfolio case study for higher education.

The goal was not just to build a UI. I wanted to show the full PM thinking behind an AI product:

- Product thesis and customer wedge
- RAG-based campus copilot concept
- AI evaluation plan
- Responsible AI guardrails
- Experiment backlog
- North-star and supporting metrics
- GTM and launch plan
- Working React + Express prototype

CampusOS explores how universities could unify student services, digital identity, credentials, admin workflows, and AI assistance into one measurable product layer.

I know this is an active space. There are already campus apps, higher-ed AI assistants, chatbots, and service automation platforms. My goal here was not to claim that the idea is completely new. My goal was to practice and demonstrate how an AI PM moves from problem framing to strategy, system design, measurement, and launch planning.

This project helped me connect engineering execution with product judgment: where AI should be used, how quality should be evaluated, what risks need guardrails, and how the product could reach a credible MVP.
