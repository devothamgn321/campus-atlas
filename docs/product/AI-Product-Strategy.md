# CampusOS AI Product Strategy

## PM Positioning

CampusOS is framed as an AI Product Manager case study: identify a fragmented campus operations problem, choose the right AI wedge, define measurable outcomes, design the system safely, and launch through a credible GTM path.

## Product Thesis

CampusOS uses AI where it compresses high-volume student uncertainty into trusted, measurable campus outcomes.

The product does not treat AI as a decorative chatbot. AI is used to:

- Reduce repetitive support load
- Guide students to the right workflow
- Ground answers in campus policy
- Surface operational bottlenecks for administrators
- Make student-service quality measurable

## Customer And Market

Primary customer:

- Mid-market and enterprise universities with fragmented student-service operations

Primary users:

- Students
- Registrar staff
- Student affairs teams
- Campus operations teams
- Faculty advisors
- University leaders

Opportunity hypothesis:

| Layer | Estimate |
|---|---|
| TAM | $18B global higher-education software and services opportunity |
| SAM | $2.4B campus workflow, student success, and service management segment |
| SOM | $24M reachable ARR across 80 institutions in first expansion market |
| Pricing | $3-6 per active student per month plus implementation and analytics add-ons |

## Strategic Wedge

Start with AI self-service for registrar, library, dining, transport, and academic FAQs. Expand into identity, workflow automation, credential verification, and admin intelligence once the product owns repeat student workflows.

## Differentiation

- Campus-specific RAG layer grounded in university policy and service data
- Closed-loop workflows instead of chatbot-only answers
- Credential-aware identity and verification layer
- Admin analytics tied to adoption, resolution quality, and operational load
- Responsible AI controls for accuracy, privacy, escalation, and auditability

## AI Use Cases

| Use case | User | PM job-to-be-done | Success metric | Guardrail |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| RAG Campus Copilot | Student | Resolve academic and service questions without waiting for staff | Verified answer resolution rate | Citations, confidence threshold, human handoff |
| Advisor Signal Engine | Faculty advisor | Identify students who need intervention before risk becomes severe | Advisor action rate | Explainability and bias monitoring |
| Credential Verifier | Registrar / verifier | Verify student records faster with tamper-evident status | Median verification time saved | Role-based access and audit trails |

## Product Principles

- Trust before automation
- Workflow completion over answer generation
- Measure outcomes, not prompt volume
- Human escalation is a product feature
- AI quality must be observable by operators
