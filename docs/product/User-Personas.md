# User Personas — CampusOS

## Persona 1: International Graduate Student

Needs:
- Quick access to campus services
- Academic planning support
- Career and research guidance
- Fewer confusing portals

Pain Points:
- Too many disconnected systems
- Difficulty finding the right department
- Manual paperwork
- Unclear campus processes

CampusOS Value:
- One interface for campus workflows
- AI copilot for guidance
- Digital identity and record access

## Persona 2: University Administrator

Needs:
- Service usage visibility
- Faster record verification
- Better operational control
- Reduced manual requests

Pain Points:
- Manual workflows
- Disconnected department data
- Limited analytics
- Repeated student queries

CampusOS Value:
- Admin dashboard
- Workflow visibility
- Centralized service layer
- Metrics-driven operations

## Persona 3: Academic Advisor

Needs:
- Student academic context
- Progress visibility
- Early risk indicators
- Better recommendation tools

Pain Points:
- Limited integrated data
- Manual advising preparation
- Hard to identify students needing support

CampusOS Value:
- Academic intelligence
- Degree planning concept
- Student progress view

## Persona 4: University Technology Leader

Needs:
- Secure architecture
- Integration-ready platform
- Scalable infrastructure
- Data governance

Pain Points:
- Legacy systems
- Vendor fragmentation
- Security and privacy concerns
- High integration cost

CampusOS Value:
- Modular architecture
- API-first roadmap
- Role-based access
- Future LMS/SIS integrations
